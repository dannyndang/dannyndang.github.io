Hey there!


I am currently a senior at UC San Diego pursuing a degree in Cognitive Science with a Speciliazation in Human-Computer Interaction.

Why design? While growing up, I noticed that I enjoyed taking a step back to observe and understand the people around me. What was it exactly that made someone happy, sad, or annoyed. And after finding that out, I wondered what I could do to make their lives better and tried to carry that out. This is why design is the perfect match for me.

As a designer and researcher, I strive to create meaningful solutions and enjoyable experiences. I find great joy in understanding and improving people's lives, and design gives me the ability to do so. I aspire to design with users in mind and to create a positive impact in the world.
